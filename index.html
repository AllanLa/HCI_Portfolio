Education

I am an Asian American and a current senior at Bucknell University studying Computer Science and Engineering! As a first generation college student pursuing the American Dream, I am passionate about using my education to give back to my community and family.

Home and Hobbies

Although Calgary, Canada is my birth place, Boston will always be home. When I was 6, my family immigrated from Canada to Boston and we've been there ever since. Growing up, I enjoyed a myriad of activities: Yu-Gi-Oh, basketball, break-dancing, tennis, gymnastics, football, and so much more! In 10th grade, I was exposed to my first computer science course and fell in love with the subject. I found a passion and decided to pursue it in college.

Aspirations

I grew up in an urban housing project and lived there most of my life. I remember having a house in Canada and one of my main goals in life is to move my family back into an actual home. I am also hoping to find a professional position where I can learn as much as possible with the eventual goal of starting and running my own company! I believe in pushing myself to reach all of my ambitions and, once I achieve them, continuing to strive to exceed those, as well.
